    📖 App Documentation

    🚀 Quick Start Guide

    1. Select Template: Use the dropdown in the configuration section to select the mode that matches your CSV structure.
    2. Download Template: Use the "Templates" menu in the header to get the correct CSV sample for your selected mode.
    3. Upload Masters: Export Ledgers, Voucher Types, and Stock Items from Tally as HTML and upload them for real-time validation.
    4. Drop & Convert: Drag your prepared CSV into the upload box. The app will validate headers and show a preview.
    5. Download XML: Click "Convert to XML" to generate a Tally-ready file.

    📋 Template Types Explained

    Single Modes (Simple/Combined)

    Best for accounting-only entries. Every row in the CSV becomes a basic ledger-to-ledger transaction.

    Multiple Modes (Simple/Combined)

    Uses the VoucherNo column to group multiple rows into a single multi-ledger voucher. Ideal for complex journals.

    Inventory Modes (Simple/Multiple)

    Advanced templates for Sales/Purchase. Supports Stock Items, Units, Quantities, Rates, 3 Tax columns, and 2 Expense columns. Handles both Sales and Purchases via the PartyDrCr column (Dr = Sales, Cr = Purchase).

    Master Creation (Ledger/Item)

    Used to bulk-create masters in Tally. Ledgers support Dr/Cr opening balances. Stock Items support opening quantities and values.

    🛠️ Inventory Specifics

    PartyDrCr column

    Specifies the transaction direction: enter Dr for Sales or Cr for Purchase. The app automatically adjusts the XML signs and ledger mappings accordingly.

    Tax & Expense Dr/Cr columns

    You can manually specify the direction for each tax and expense ledger using the TaxDrCr and ExpDrCr columns. If left empty, the app automatically assigns the opposite direction of the Party (e.g., if Party is Dr, Tax/Exp is Cr).

    Tax & Expense Aggregation

    In "Inventory Multiple" mode, if you have multiple rows with the same Voucher Number, the app will sum up all Tax and Expense amounts to create a clean, consolidated voucher in Tally.

    📁 Master Creation Guide

    Bulk Ledger Creation

    Use the Create Ledger (Master) template. Specify the Parent group (e.g., Sundry Creditors) and DrCr for opening balance. Tally will automatically create these ledgers in your company.

    Bulk Stock Item Creation

    Use the Create Stock Item (Master) template. Ensure you upload your Units HTML from Tally first, so the app can verify that the UOM (Units of Measure) exist in your Tally company before conversion.

    📥 How to Upload in Tally

    For Transactions (Sales, Purchase, etc.)
    1. Go to Import Data (Alt+O) > Transactions.
    2. Provide the path to your XML file and Enter.
    For Masters (Ledger, Stock Item)
    1. Go to Import Data (Alt+O) > Masters.
    2. Select Modify with new data to ensure opening balances are updated.
    3. Provide the path to your XML file and Enter.

    📤 How do I export Masters from Tally?

    1. Go to Display More Reports > Statement of Accounts > Statistics.
    2. On Right Side Select type of master & Enter to open.
    3. Press F5 (Ledger View or Stock Item View) to show all details.
    4. Press Export (Ctrl+E).
    5. Select HTML format and save the file.

    ❓ FAQs & Troubleshooting

    Q: Why do I get "Template Mismatch" error?

    A: This happens if the CSV you uploaded doesn't have the columns required for the mode you selected. Double-check your mode selection or download the template again.

    Q: How do I handle multiple items in one invoice?

    A: Use the Inventory Multiple template. Give all items belonging to the same invoice the same VoucherNo.

    Q: Tally says "Voucher totals do not match", what do I do?

    A: Ensure your Item Amount + Tax + Expenses equals the total value assigned to the Party Ledger. The app calculates this automatically, so check your CSV math.

    Q: Can I use custom Voucher Types?

    A: Yes. Just make sure the Voucher Type name in your CSV exactly matches the name of the Voucher Type in Tally (case-insensitive).

    Q: Is my data safe?

    A: 100%. This is a client-side application. No data is ever uploaded to a server; everything happens locally in your browser.

    🛡️ Privacy Policy

    100% Client-Side Processing

    CSVtoTally is designed with privacy as its core principle. Unlike other converters, all data processing—parsing your CSV, validating against your Tally masters, and generating the final XML—happens entirely within your web browser.

    No Data Uploads

    Your financial data, ledger names, and voucher details are never transmitted to any server. We do not have access to your files, nor do we store any of your data on our systems.

    Local Storage Usage

    We use your browser's sessionStorage to temporarily remember your uploaded Tally Masters (Ledgers, Items, etc.) so you don't have to re-upload them if you refresh the page. This data is cleared when you close your browser tab.

    External Resources

    This app loads Google Fonts for typography and PapaParse (via CDN) for CSV handling. These external services do not receive any of the data you process within the app.
